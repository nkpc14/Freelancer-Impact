Pre-inspections of Automobiles & Machinery

Before accepting a premium and drawing up any insurance contract with the customer (i.e. vehicle owner) , insurance companies demand ‘Pre-inspection Report’ of vehicle & 'Risk-inspection Report' in case of machineries. This report is demanded to avoid any future disputes in case of claim arrival and therefore helps in minimizing the loss. This task is executed by Third Party Agencies to ensure a fair opinion, which in turns aids in getting the quality & filtered business. as well as reduces admin work of insurance company. The inspection report thus plays a vital role in risk minimization and ensures a speedy closure. This report is necessary in following situations:

  • Roll Over Cases – When the customer shifts from one insurance company to another.
  • Break-In Insurance – When there is a lapse in the policy, i.e. break-in policy issuance.
  • In case of bi-fuel fitment or accessories fitment – Where there is a bi-fuel fitment i.e. CNG/LPG fitted in vehicle or accessories such as music system, LCD etc. fitted and which needs to be insured.
