ABOUT US

Impact Surveyors (I) Pvt. Ltd. is a foremost agency whose services are employed by major insurance companies to inspect automobile & machineries for insurance policy issuance as well as evaluate, investigate & process in case of claims.

Embarking on this unchartered field of surveying vehicular insurance are a group of qualified engineers poised to provide efficient and user-friendly services in the arena of Pre-Inspection, Motor Theft & O.D. claim Investigation & Claim Process (Motor).

Combining modern methods of evaluation with their collective expertise, Impact Surveyors (I) Pvt Ltd. offer key solutions in these areas of insurance and thereof.

IRDA Accreditation:

All directors hold an individual accreditation from Insurance Regulatory Development Authority ( IRDA ). Skilled in Motor Survey and Loss Assessment fields, the team also boasts of an impressive infiltration into Motor Claim process (Claims & Investigation).

Impartial and Punctual:

Impact Surveyors (I) Pvt Ltd., is a syndicate which prides in keeping itself informed in automobile technologies and updated with any developments in this sphere. As a result, Impact Surveyors(I) PvtLtd., works efficiently to complete the process in “Fast and Fair” means with their cutting edge solutions.
